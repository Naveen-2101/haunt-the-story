THE STORY

# H A U N T # T H E S T O R Y ## CHAPTER: 1 ### PART: 1 One day, I was completely alone at home and feeling bored. Nothing was coming to my mind. Then I called my friends and said, “Guys, let’s go to that old abandoned school.” As soon as Ansh heard this, he became nervous. He said, “I heard there’s a girl’s spirit there… one that cries near the bushes in the school garden.” Harsh, Sai, and Ansh’s brother Ayush also said they had heard the same rumor, but they thought it was just a myth. We all convinced Ansh together, and after some time, he finally agreed. I said, “Meet at the square in 30 minutes.” Thirty minutes later, we all met at the square and started walking toward the old school. As soon as we reached near the school, Sai felt something strange. Suddenly, an old man ran past us shouting, “Don’t go there! She will kill all of you!” Ansh got terrified and said, “I already told you… let’s just go home.” I laughed and said, “There’s nothing there. Looks like the old man has lost his mind because of age. Come on, let’s go inside.” We entered the school. Inside, there was complete silence. Broken benches, burned blackboards, a strange smell in the air, and an icy cold wind surrounded the place. While we were exploring, we suddenly heard a dragging sound coming from the third floor. We went upstairs. The higher we climbed, the louder the sound became. It was coming from one classroom. Ansh completely panicked and ran away. His brother Ayush went with him. At that moment, Harsh got a call from his mother. She needed him for some work, so he left too. Now only Sai and I were left inside the school. Suddenly, the glass of a classroom window shattered on its own. We both screamed and ran outside toward the garden. Darkness had started spreading around the school. Then we heard a strange crying sound coming from near the bushes… exactly like the one Ansh had described earlier. Sai froze in fear. At that moment, I remembered everything Ansh had told us. Suddenly, a girl emerged from the bushes. Her hair completely covered her face. She slowly started floating in the air, and then we finally saw her face. Blood was flowing from her eyes. Both of us were horrified. Gathering some courage, I asked, “Who are you?” She gave no reply. Instead, she slowly stretched her hand toward Sai. Suddenly, Sai’s eyes turned completely red, and he started floating in the air. Then the girl spoke, “You troubled me the most… I will never spare you.” And the very next second… she disappeared. Sai fell to the ground unconscious. I tried to wake him up. After some time, he opened his eyes. Both of us ran toward the school gate. The same old man was standing there again. He said, “I warned you… don’t go there. She will kill you.” I dropped Sai at his house and returned home. That night, I couldn’t sleep at all. My mind was filled with questions. **Do ghosts really exist?** Why didn’t that girl harm me? Why did she choose only Sai? Did Sai do something before entering the school… or inside it… that we failed to notice? If the girl was only a rumor… then how did we see her? Was the old man only trying to warn us… or did he have some connection with that girl? If the school was abandoned… then how did the old man know everything? Who was that old man… and how did he know so much? These endless questions kept me awake the whole night. I was sitting on my chair near my PC when suddenly my phone rang. As soon as I answered the call, I heard a cold breath… followed by the sound of someone crying. It was a woman’s voice… but there was no sadness in that crying. It felt more like a warning. The crying sound slowly started coming closer… as if it wasn’t coming from the phone anymore, but from somewhere inside my house. Then a voice whispered through the phone. The voice was so faint that I couldn’t fully understand the words. I could only hear: “Don’t turn around…” But I turned around. At the corner of my bed, something was standing there. The moment I saw it, my entire body went numb. It was the same girl we had seen in the school. She looked exactly the same. Then she slowly started floating toward me. I couldn’t move at all. I couldn’t even scream. Suddenly, my room door opened and my sister walked inside. The girl vanished instantly. I couldn’t sleep for the rest of the night. The next morning, I called Harsh nervously and told him that everything Ansh had said about the school was true. Sai and I had seen everything with our own eyes. Seeing how terrified I sounded, Harsh told me to come to Ayush’s house so everyone could carefully listen to what happened. Later, all of us — me, Sai, Harsh, Ayush, and Ansh — gathered there. Sai and I told them everything that had happened in the school. I also told them what happened to me the previous night. Everyone was shocked after hearing it. Ansh said, “Your sister saved you last night.” I replied, “Yeah… I think so too.” We discussed everything for a long time. Soon all of us became worried about what would happen if that spirit attacked us again. Then Ayush said, “Do you all remember that old man who warned us outside the school? We should find him. He probably knows everything.” But Ansh quickly said, “No. We should never go there again. That place is dangerous. What if that ghost attacks us again?” I agreed with Ansh because deep down, I was terrified too. Then Harsh said, “It’s okay. We’ll discuss this another time. For now, everyone should just go home.” After that, we all returned to our houses. Two days later, I got a call from Ayush. He sounded extremely terrified. In a trembling voice, he said, “Aryan… Sai is possessed.” Hearing this, my entire body froze. I asked, “What? How did this happen? What’s going on?” Ayush replied nervously, “I’ll explain everything later. First, come to Sai’s house with Harsh. Hurry.” I immediately called Harsh, and both of us rushed to Sai’s house. When we entered, I saw a priest performing an exorcism on Sai. Sai was floating in the air, screaming violently. He shouted in a horrifying voice, “Where are the other four boys who came to the school?! I remember one of their names… Aryan! Where is he?! I will never spare any of them!” Blood was flowing from Sai’s body… even from his eyes. Seeing all this made me completely numb with fear. Then Sai suddenly looked at me and stretched his hand toward me. Instantly, I froze in place. It felt as if something invisible had grabbed my entire body. I couldn’t move at all. Slowly… I started floating in the air too. Then the priest began chanting loudly, and after a few moments, I fell back to the ground. After that, the priest threw something at Sai, and he suddenly became calm. They tied him to a bed inside a room and locked the door. Then the priest came to me and said, “She only wants you.” I asked, “Why?” He replied, “I’ll tell you later. First, we must go back to that school.” The next evening, all of us — me, Harsh, Ayush, and Ansh — went near the school with the priest. The same old man appeared again and shouted, “Don’t go inside! The spirit is furious! She won’t spare any of you!” We ignored him once again. But deep inside, I wanted to ask him who he really was. Still, I stayed silent. We entered the school. For some time, everything seemed normal. But then once again, we heard sounds coming from the third floor. We went upstairs, and I showed the priest the classroom where everything had happened before. The priest started chanting inside the classroom. That was when I noticed something. Under one of the benches, deep within the darkness, two glowing eyes were staring at us. Whatever it was… it was watching us without moving, without blinking. Half of its face was hidden. The shape looked twisted and unnatural… barely human. There wasn’t just anger in its eyes. There was hunger. And the most terrifying part was that the moment our eyes met… it slowly smiled at me. Suddenly, a cracking sound echoed from beneath the bench, like bones twisting out of place. Its hand slowly crawled out. The fingers were unnaturally long, scratching the floor as if it was preparing to crawl toward us. My breathing stopped. I couldn’t move at all. I screamed and pointed toward it, telling everyone what I saw. But when the priest looked there… nothing was there anymore. We stepped out into the hallway. Suddenly, a black flying figure rushed toward us and attacked the priest violently. He was thrown across the hallway. We ran toward him. The priest quickly started chanting again and shouted, “Run! Get out of here now!” Then that black flying creature returned once again… and this time, it dragged the priest away into the darkness.
